# Kalunga V25

This is a decent step up in difficulty compared to V20, but wtill very doable in a single KI entry.  The party composition we used was the following:

```
PLD COR BRD WHM WAR SAM
```

This is not set-in-stone.  You can substitue the **SAM** with a **BLU**, and a **WAR** with a **DRK**.  Just play to what you have.

## Buffs

**COR**: Crooked `Chaos`, `Samurai`\
**WHM**: `Protect`, `Shell`, `Barfira`, `Barviruna`, `Haste`, `Auspice`, `Boost-STR`, `Regen`\
**BRD**: `Soul Voice`, `Clarion Call` (Do not use NiTro yet):
1. `Dirge`
2. `Honor`
3. `Minuet 5`
4. `Minuet 4`
5. `Minuet 3`
6. `Sirvente` → **PLD**

## Fight

**PLD** needs to get hate and the rest should engage from behind Kalunga.  

**BRD** needs to redo songs once there is 1 minute remaining on `Soul Voice`.  Pop NiTro and redo songs as follows:

(Next to WHM):
1. `Dirge`
2. `Honor`
3. `Minne 5`
4. `Ballad 3`
5. `Ballad 2`

(Next to melee):
1. `Dirge`
2. `Honor`
3. `Minuet 5`
4. `Minuet 4`
5. Either `Minuet 3`, `Minne 5`, or `Aria` - your choice.

Debuffs:
1. `Elegy` → **Kalunga**
2. `Requiem` → **Kalunga**
3. `Elegy` → **Adds** (when they pop)

Tank:
1. `Sirvente` → **PLD**
2. `Victory March` → **PLD**
3. `Fire Carol` → **PLD**
4. `Ballad 3` → **PLD** (optional)

The **BRD** absolutely must have dedicated macros for `Pianissimo` songs on the **PLD**.  They will be *frequently* dispelled throughout this fight, and will need re-buffed constantly.  Don't fret about `Soul Voice` songs on the **PLD**.

Similarly, the **WHM** needs to be on top of buff loss.  **PLD** should help cure when `Majesty` is up.

Due to needing to know which songs have been dispelled it is **highly recommended** that the **BRD** use the `PartyBuffs` addon with color coded song icons to quickly see what is missing.

## Fetters & Mechinics
**Blistering Roar** will open the Red <span style="color:red">**!!**</span> window and spawn fetters.  You have two options for this fight, and both have their pros and cons.  

### Remove the Fetters (Blue <span style="color:blue">**!!**</span>)

You can remove the fetters and the `Burn` aura by initiating a multi-step skillchain of an increasing length depending on how many times he's entered fetter mode.  It is very important that your DD's are in communication and coordinated enough to stop their normal WS cadence and yield to a multi-step SC.  If you are using a **SAM**, this is fairly easy to do with **Tachi: Jinpu** spam.  Avoid doing any skillchain that does fire-elemental damage, as that will heal **Kalunga** for 99,999 during this period.  That includes: `Liquefaction`, `Fusion`, and `Light`.

Once the blue <span style="color:blue">**!!**</span> has been hit, you can resume the normal WS cadence.

### Ignore the Fetters

You can also elect to completely ignore the fetters.  This is ostensibly a more difficult strategy, but there is a very big advantage to utilizing this method: 
> **Kalunga** cannot use **Incinerating Lahar** 

If you are going to employ this method, the **PLD** and **WHM** absolutely must be able to withstand the halos and nasty `Burn` effect.  `Burn` not only is a massive DoT, but it gives a staggering **-999 INT** to anyone unfortunate enough to be afflicted with its effects.  If positioned correctly, this should generally only be affecting the **PLD**, but that makes the tank's job (and by extension, the **WHM** and **BRD** as well) much more demanding.

I do not recommend this method lightly.  Only take this route if you are sure of your abilities to survive.  Locking him out of using **Incinerating Lahar** is a massive game changer and significantly increases your chances of success, but only if you are able to survive a constant fetter.

This isn't too difficult of a fight - the largest issue for this one is the time restriction and getting lucky on auras and **Incinerating Lahar** usage.  We have gotten him down to 20% with 7 minutes left on the clock, but lost from timeout due to him spamming **Incinerating Lahar**.

Keep at it, and you'll get a win!
