# Xevioso V25
This guy just sucks.  There isn't much in the way as far as mechanics go, he just has a lot of ways to end your run.  I have quite a few recordings of attempts that look like they're going great, until we're all just dead in 2 seconds.

The single worst aura to get on him is **Magic Evasion Down**.  He will absolutely cripple everyone if you get this aura.

The suggested party composition for this is:
> WHM BRD COR GEO WAR DNC

**Every single person needs 2 stacks of `Panacea` and a macro to use them**

## Buffs
**COR**: `Gallant`, Crooked `Chaos`, `Samurai`

**WHM**: `Protect`, `Shell`, `Baraera`, `Barsilence`, `Haste`, `Auspice`, `Boost-STR`, `Regen`

**BRD**: Wait for **COR** to do the second roll. `Soul Voice`, `Clarion Call`, `NiTro`:
1. `Scherzo`
2. `Wind Carol 2`
3. `Ballad 3`
4. `Minne 5`
5. `Honor March`

Pause here and let the **WHM** run out of range, then continue:

6. `Valor Minuet 4`
7. `Valor Minuet 5`

If done correctly, the WHM should have `Scherzo`, `Carol`, `Ballad`, `Minne`, `Honor`, and the rest should have `Scherzo`, `Carol`, `Honor`, `Minuet`, `Minuet`.

If you can fit `Aria` in somewhere, that would be great, but other than `Minuet 4` or `Honor March`, I don't see what is a viable drop option.

After the songs are up, the **BRD** needs to run up and pull **Xevioso** down to the bottom of the stairs.  `NiTro` needs to still be active to apply `Ice Threnody II` and `Pining Nocturne` on the pull.

## Fight & Mechanics
Fan out in a circle around **Xevioso**.  Everyone is a tank here, and it's critical that people aren't stacked in conal ranges.  **Xevioso** has some nasty debuffs that you will need to have a macro at the ready to use **Panacea** or **Remedy** to remove them immediately.  The **WHM** is going to be extremely busy keeping everyone alive, and will not have time to dedicate to casting `Erase` or whatver `-na` spell you require.

The **GEO** needs to use `Geo-Fury` and `Indi-Barrier`.

He is weak to piercing attacks, so the **WAR** should use polearm.  

**Droning Whirlwind** will open red <span style="color:red">**!!**</span> and open a fetter window.  To remove the fetters, you need to do a number of **Critical Hits**.  Using `Stardiver` to lower his critical hit evasion, and using multi-hit weaponskills like `Evisceration` is a quick way to get a blue <span style="color:blue">**!!**</span> proc to remove the fetters.

He has particularly nasty status ailments that he can apply.  `Zombie` and `Weakness` are the worst to get.  This is what makes **Magic Evasion Down** aura a run-killer.

Overall, he's not too bad of a fight.  As with most of the V25's, it comes down to aura luck and good coordination.
